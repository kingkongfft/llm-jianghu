# Claude 3.5 Sonnet

**Claude 3.5 Sonnet** is Anthropic's mid-tier yet high-performance model, released in June 2024. It quickly became a favorite among developers for its exceptional coding abilities and speed, often outperforming the previous flagship, Claude 3 Opus.

## Technical Specifications

- **Release Date**: June 20, 2024
- **Developer**: [Anthropic](../companies/Anthropic.md)
- **Architecture**: Transformer-based
- **Context Window**: 200,000 tokens
- **Output Limit**: 8,192 tokens
- **Knowledge Cutoff**: April 2024

## Key Features

- **Coding Proficiency**: Widely considered the best model for coding tasks upon release, solving 64% of internal agentic coding evaluation problems.
- **Artifacts**: Launched alongside the "Artifacts" UI feature, allowing the model to generate and render code, documents, and diagrams in a dedicated window.
- **Speed**: Operates at twice the speed of Claude 3 Opus while being significantly cheaper.
- **Vision**: Strongest vision capabilities in the Claude 3 family at launch.

## Impact

Claude 3.5 Sonnet shifted the industry perception of "mid-tier" models, proving that a smaller, faster model could outperform larger "frontier" models from just a few months prior. Its integration with the "Artifacts" UI pioneered a new way of interacting with LLMs, moving beyond simple chat to collaborative creation.
